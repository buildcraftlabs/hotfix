How It Works

Set it and forget it.

Three steps. No configuration required. Just install and let Hotfix watch your back.

1

Monitor

Every 5 seconds, Hotfix runs ps to sample CPU usage for every running process on your Mac.

2

Detect

If a process exceeds your CPU threshold (default 80%) and stays there for the kill duration (default 60s), it's flagged for termination.

3

Kill

A SIGTERM signal is sent. A macOS notification tells you what was terminated and why. Your fans spin down. You carry on.
