How It Works

Simple. Automatic. Quiet.

Hotfix runs silently in your system tray and handles everything without interrupting your work.

1

Monitor

Hotfix polls all running processes every 5 seconds, tracking CPU usage per process over time.

2

Detect

When a process sustains CPU usage above your threshold for longer than your configured duration, it gets flagged.

3

Kill

The offending process is terminated. You get a notification. Your fan slows down. Done.
